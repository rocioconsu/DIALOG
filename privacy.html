Privacy & Data Ethics

Commitment to Transparency

At DIALOG, we believe that the future of interactive systems depends on trust. As technology moves toward more immersive experiences like facial mapping and RFID tracking, ethical considerations become the cornerstone of design. Our commitment to privacy is absolute. This platform is an educational resource; therefore, we do not store personal identifiers, biometric data from AR simulations, or location history.

Privacy by Design

We adhere to 'Privacy by Design' principles, ensuring that every interactive module is a safe space for exploration. We advocate for transparency, ensuring users are always aware of how their data is being used to enhance their experience. This includes being clear about when a camera is being used for Augmented Reality or how movement is tracked in a Phygital environment.

Academic Scope

All information provided follows the highest standards of digital ethics and data protection regulations. DIALOG does not share data with third parties and does not use cookies for commercial tracking. Our goal is strictly to bridge the gap between graphic design and technological implementation while protecting the user's digital footprint.
